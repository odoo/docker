View/Print Label  
 
  1. Print the label:   Select Print from the File menu in this browser window to print the label below.

  2. Customs Invoice   - 3 copies of a completed customs invoice are required for shipments with a commercial value being shipped to/from non-EU countries. Please insure the customs invoice contains address information, product detail - including value, shipment date and your signature.

  3. Fold the printed label at the dotted line.   Place the label in a UPS Shipping Pouch. If you do not have a pouch, affix the folded label using clear plastic shipping tape over the entire label.

  4. Pickup and Drop-off
    • Daily Pickup customers: Have your shipment(s) ready for the driver as usual.
    • To Schedule a Pickup or to find a drop-off location, select the Pickup or Drop-off icon from the tool bar.
